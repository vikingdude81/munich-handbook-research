# Analysis of Writeback Logs and RESEARCH_MANIFEST.md

## Objective
To inspect the most recent writeback logs for patterns in errors and cross-reference them with `RESEARCH_MANIFEST.md`.

## Steps

1. **Analyze the log files** for repeated chunk IDs and specific error types.
2. **Review `RESEARCH_MANIFEST.md`** to identify any chunks flagged as high-priority or ambiguous.
3. **Compare findings** from step 1 and step 2 to understand potential issues and prioritize them accordingly.

## Purpose
This analysis helps identify:
- Chunks that may have failed processing
- Patterns in errors that could indicate systematic issues
- High-priority chunks that need attention based on manifest flags
